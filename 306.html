Let's go have some fun.

Alex Yang

27 Jul 2018

Oh fuck it let's go have some fun.

HONNE is a special band. I first heard them through discover weekly sometime during high school, I remember walking home past Gillespie Field and "Someone that loves you" started playing.

This song witnessed one of the biggest changes to my inner self up to this point in life. I was in a rough place, coming back from Shanghai to Beijing, trying to find some sort of purpose to my life. This song filled my late night orange light filled walks through the dark alleys, slightly drunk from whisky and high on life. I don't know what a 306 is, but I'm assuming it's a car. "You probably heard me when I drove right past, windows down money trees on full blast." I can totally imagine this, a shitty ass car blasting loud music during late nights. This song embodied my anticipation of college. It was a bit over a month before the start of college and I couldn't wait. The car was his tool to his freedom, to go anywhere and everywhere. My freedom was so close.

Listening to HONNE live in Beijing, as this song came on, I cried. I screamed my lungs out the entire song, it was the first time I have ever cried with joy. It was total bliss.

You probably knew me more than anyone here
Well you were pretty much my teenage years

The car in this case to me could also be seen as my sadness and sorrows, which followed me throughout my life no matter what. I guess that's the same with everyone, I think it is true that my sadness knew me more than anyone here.

This song is one of my 9 fav favs songs, it captured a moment in my timeline where I was actually excited for the future.

Not a love song yay!!!!!
