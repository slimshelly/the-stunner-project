na·dir
/'nādər,ˈnādir/
noun

noun: nadir; plural noun: nadirs
the lowest point in the fortunes of a person or organization.

This song was actually so dramatically timed for my life, I felt I probably did reach the lowest point of my life at the start of my time in Beijing summer 2018. I had just started working at Bricks, I felt lost, lonely and bored all the time. Nothing was happening.

TENDER stuck with me during my highs and lows in Shanghai as well, I can still picture me stumbling back to my room at 1 or 2 AM, drunk on cocktails and blasting this song. Nadir talks about the end of an relationship where both parties resent each other.

This song is not too abstract but seriously beautifully written, they are at this point only staying together for the appearances and comfort of having someone, there is no love.

Who're we kidding but ourselves? This is the end.
