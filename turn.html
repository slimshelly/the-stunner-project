First up, Turn by The Wombats.

Alex Yang

15 Jul 2018

The starting guitar bit sounds to me just like an engine starting, the album cover is drawn so elegantly, I can just imagine driving at night and blasting this song.

"Turn" to me, is a love song (no shit). It illustrates the ups and downs of a relationship, of a slice of life that makes you feel alive. I like the way you turn me inside and out, maybe it's the crazy that I'd miss, baby it's the crazy I like.

-------

"Turn" is what a relationship should be like to me, it doesn't have to be all smooth sailing, and it doesn't have to be your soul mate or your true love whatever, but it should make you mad and crazy, and it should make you feel alive, where when you look back on it, the good and the bad, the rough and the smooth, it happened, and that's what life is. You're in for a ride. Watering plastic plants, smashing your phone, you're going insane because of this person.
